Chapter Leadership

Sarah Maeng

Chapter President

Sarah is a sophomore at Taipei American School. Moved by the impact of domestic violence and inspired to make a difference, she started a chapter of The Safe Nest Project to bring the nonprofit to Taiwan and support children affected by domestic violence in her community. Outside of the nonprofit, Sarah is a national circuit policy debater, loves spending time with friends and family, and enjoys playing badminton.
